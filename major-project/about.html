ABOUT

In an effort to transform cybersecurity education, this research examines the conception, execution, and effects of an animated cyber-e-learning website. The study explores the special qualities and capabilities of the platform, emphasizing how dynamic visual content and interactive modules can improve learning outcomes. The paper opens with a summary of cybersecurity education as it is today and the growing need for interesting and useful learning resources. After that, it presents the animated cyber e-learning website, going into its design, user interface, and utilization of animated content to make difficult cybersecurity ideas understandable. This study attempts to offer important insights into the efficacy and viability of animated cyber e-learning using a combination of user feedback, case studies, and comparison analyses with traditional e-learning approaches. The results add to the current conversation on creative methods of teaching cybersecurity by providing suggestions to educators, developers, and legislators who want to improve students' knowledge in this important area.
